Voice helpline (demo)

For elderly residents without a smartphone, LocalPulse runs a phone line. Call in your language, ask about roads, shelters, power, water, medical help, or get connected to 112.

Your speech stays on your device (browser Web Speech for listening and speaking). Your words go to an agent that reasons over live satellite, ground and official data, remembers the conversation, uses your location, and can act for you with your spoken confirmation. One model: Gemini Flash-Lite.
